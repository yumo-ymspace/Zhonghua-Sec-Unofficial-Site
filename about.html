About Us

Our Story & Purpose

Founded in 1911 as Chung Hwa Girls' School, Zhonghua has evolved into a co-educational autonomous Special Assistance Plan (SAP) school.

👨‍🏫

Mr Tan Kim Koon

Principal

Message for 2026: Remember, Return, Reignite

"For more than a century, generations of Zhonghuarians have walked through our gates, carrying with them hopes and aspirations. 2026 marks a significant milestone as we commemorate our 115th anniversary."

"We remain committed to strengthening what matters most: teaching and learning, student well-being, and a caring school environment. We will sharpen our strategic approach through the 4L framework: Life-long Learning, Life-wide Learning, Life-wise Learning, and Life-strong Learning."

"Together, let us remember our past, return to our roots, and reignite the spirit that defines us."
